Managed Print Services

Managed Print Services is a package that takes care of an organisations printing requirements with the purpose of saving them time, money and resources.

Results can be demonstrated by analysing all print requirements thereby providing the client with more visibility and control of their printing environment.

Managed Print Services includes the management of all print devices such as printers, scanners, copiers and multifunctional devices which may include document workflow solutions such as papercut.
